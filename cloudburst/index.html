About Us

Since 2006, Cloudburst & Present Trading Inc. has been a trusted leader in the Philippine meat industry, bridging the gap between quality and affordability.

We specialize in the seamless import and export of premium commodities, empowering local and international businesses to thrive. Beyond trade, we are committed to making a tangible impact—helping our partners fulfill their corporate social responsibility while strengthening the communities we serve.
