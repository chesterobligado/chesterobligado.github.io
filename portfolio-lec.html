Legazpi Eye Center: A Modernized Online Presence

I collaborated with Studio Hibang to redesign and redevelop the Legazpi Eye Center website, creating a modern, user-friendly platform that aligns with their updated brand guidelines. The goal was to enhance the overall patient experience by providing a seamless digital solution.

I created a sleek and contemporary design that reflects the clinic's commitment to advanced eye care. I also streamlined the user interface, making it easy for patients to find the information they need. Additionally, I implemented a convenient online booking system that allows patients to schedule appointments based on their preferred doctor, date, time, and clinic location.

By implementing these features, I was able to improve the overall user experience and streamline the appointment booking process for Legazpi Eye Center's patients. This project showcases my ability to create functional and visually appealing websites that meet the specific needs of clients and their users.
