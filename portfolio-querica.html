Querica: Elevating the Pili Nut Experience

I collaborated with Studio Hibang to elevate Querica's brand and provide a seamless online shopping experience. The project involved designing and developing a range of innovative packaging solutions and a user-friendly e-commerce platform.

I designed and developed visually appealing and functional packaging for the 50G canisters, foil packaging, sampler boxes, and special flavor boxes. Additionally, I created a user-friendly Shopify e-commerce website, featuring a clean and modern design, intuitive navigation, and seamless checkout process.

By combining strategic design with technical expertise, I helped The Querica create a strong brand identity and a thriving online presence.
