Built on Quality. Growing with Purpose.

Founded in 2021, Golden Tiger Unlimited OPC began as a response to a clear and growing need — Filipinos with discerning tastes and high standards were underserved when it came to premium imported food products.

We started small, but with a clear sense of direction. Our focus was never on volume — it was on quality. Every product we bring to market is carefully selected, thoughtfully sourced, and held to standards we are proud to stand behind.

As a growing company, we understand that trust is earned, not given. That is why we pour our energy into every client relationship, every product selection, and every delivery. Because for us, excellence isn’t just an aspiration. It’s the foundation we’re building on.

Mission

To make the world's finest imported food products accessible to the Philippine market — delivering quality, authenticity, and exceptional value to every client we serve.

Vision

To grow into the Philippines' most trusted name in premium imported products — one relationship, one delivery, and one exceptional product at a time.
